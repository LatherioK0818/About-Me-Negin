Negin's Life Journey

My name is Negin, and I'm a 32-year-old originally from Iran. My life has been a series of courageous leaps and transformative chapters. At the tender age of 18, I ventured alone to Malaysia to pursue higher education, a journey that required immense courage and independence. At 21, my family and I made a life-altering decision to immigrate to the United States, where I found myself juggling full-time work in the challenging field of banking while diligently pursuing my education part-time.

five years of dedicated service in the banking industry, I embarked on a new adventure by enlisting in the U.S. Army, where I devoted three years to active duty, serving my country. This commitment wasn't without its trials, as I sustained an injury during my service. However, my unwavering determination carried me through, and I recently received an honorable discharge from the military.

I'm setting my sights on a long-held dream. With a passion for technology, I'm beginning a new journey as a software developer. This exciting career transition reflects not only my aspirations but also my determination to continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible. My life's journey has been an ever-evolving story of resilience, adaptability, and relentless pursuit of my dreams, and I'm excited to see where this new chapter takes me.
